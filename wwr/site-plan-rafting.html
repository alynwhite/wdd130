White Water Site Plan

Overview

Purpose

We are committed to bringing novice, hobbiest to the experienced white water river rafters onto the water. We understand the stress of booking vacations and trips and we want to mitigate as much stress as possible. To do this, we went online so you can, manage/book trips, find deals and open the opportunites of rafting all over the western United States. We are committed to the community of rafting.

Audience

Target Customers

Customer 1

Customer 1 is an outoor enthusiast who is 25. They love to be outside going on secluded hikes that takes a few days to finish. They also like hiking the Grand Canyon from rim to rim. This customer is looking for an extreme trip on the river but secluded from a ton of tourists. This person is currently working remotely so they can spend time in their camper seeing the best that mother nature offers.

Customer 2

Customer 2 is a middle age mom in charge of booking a trip for a youth church trip. The group is on a budget and the youth are teenagers, 13-18 years of age. The mom is looking up trips on her phone while she sits in a meeting trying to figure out a company that the dates will work with hers. She wants to know all the options that she has. If she has a good experience with this website, she will definitely share it with her sister who lives in Wyoming who is in the same situation as her.

Customer 3

Customer 3 is traveling to an area known for white water river rafting. They are tourists in their 40's wanting to raft but aren't sure what there agenda will be like. They want to know the prices and type of trips there are for river rafting. They are on the computer looking up places to go rafting. They find this website. They want to know price, times and places to river raft.

How will the audience usally access my site?

.

Most people use their phones now days so the website will be very mobile friendly. There are some people who use laptop/desktop but the inital use will be mobile.

What does the audience want?

They will want to find and book river rafting trips through our website.
